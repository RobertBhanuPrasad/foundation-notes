**LLD** :

HLD : It is the outline of the system architecture overview of buleprint of each module and their intractions.

LLD: Means in detailed architecture of each module, internal structure, implementation details - It is a detailed blueprint, that developers can directly implement.

step by step explaination of LLD process:

1) Review of the high level design to understadn the system architecture, major components, and their interactions.
2) Break down system into smaller modules or classess that perform specific functions.
3) Designe the class and object structure.
4) Defind how mudles communicates with each other through method signatures, input/output parameters and protocols.
5) Design the data structure and algorithem to omplemente the modules functionality and efficiently.
6) Create the diagrams and documentation to represent the design
7) Consider the error hanlding, validations and edge cases.
8) Collaborate with peers and architects to refind the design before implementation.

**Why LLD** :

1) Provides developers with a clear, actionable blueprint.
2) Encourage developers to write moduler, reusable and maintainable code.
3) Detailed desgin helps in writing precise unit tests.
4) Clarifies responsibilities and interactions between the modules.
5) Servers a communication tool among developers and stakeholders.
